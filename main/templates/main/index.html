Quezon City Animal Care and Adoption Center

The Quezon City Government, through the city’s Animal Welfare and Rehabilitation Program, has established an adoption center for impounded and neglected animals. The 450-square meter pet adoption center in Barangay Payatas can provide temporary homes for up to 60 animals until they find new families. The facility also includes a surgery room for dogs and cats that need to be neutered and spayed, as well as for other animals requiring immediate medical attention. Two separate cages are available to isolate sick animals from healthy ones, preventing the spread of infections. Before being offered for adoption, the animals will undergo rehabilitation. This comprehensive approach marks the city's commitment to rehabilitating animals and finding them new homes.
