Quezon City Animal Care and Adoption Center

Welcome to our animal shelter, where compassion takes center stage. Here, we delve into the vital role of animal care and adoption centers, institutions dedicated to providing shelter, medical care, and adoption programs for stray or abandoned animals. Beyond these crucial services, we actively engage in activities aimed at promoting responsible pet ownership and fostering awareness about pressing animal welfare issues. Join us on this journey of care, support, and advocacy for our beloved furry companions.

Available Programs

Individual Adoption Program

Facilitating the placement of companion animals into loving homes through a personalized and compassionate adoption process.

Emotional Support Animals Program

Providing individuals with therapeutic companionship by connecting them with trained animals to alleviate emotional or psychological challenges.

Pet Education Program

Promoting responsible pet ownership and enhancing the well-being of animals through educational initiatives that cover proper care, nutrition, and understanding animal behavior.

Veterinary Response Team

Mobilizing a skilled and rapid-response veterinary team to address and manage emergencies, ensuring the health and welfare of animals in crisis situations.
